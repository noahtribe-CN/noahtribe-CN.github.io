近未来世界观
Near future world view

     
地球资源枯竭问题没能得到根本性的解决，人类后代在严酷的生存环境据此重新建构替代方案，
人工智能系统Nuwa为新人类开发了数据化视觉系统、方位立体声感知系统、生物方位触觉感知系统。
先进科技与原始意识共存的新疆界由此拓展，新的联觉系统在她们体内逐渐形成…… 


The problem of the depletion of the earth’s resources has not been fundamentally solved, and the descendants of mankind
have reconstructed alternative plans based on this in the harsh living environment.Nuwa, an artificial intelligence system,
has developed a digital vision system,
an orientation stereo perception system, and a biological orientation tactile perception system for new humans.
The Xinjiang realm where advanced technology and primitive consciousness coexist has thus expanded,
and a new synesthetic system has gradually formed in their bodies...
Will humans stop at the five senses?

可穿戴设备构想
Wearable device concept


当下我们的所见所听逐步被数字内容主导，
人体的现实感官不断接收着虚拟的感知刺激，但“所感，即所在”吗？
At present, what we see and hear is gradually dominated by digital content.
The real sense organs of the human body continue to receive virtual perceptual stimulation,
but is "what we feel is where we are"?

在此次媒介感知课程中，我们将 “数字内容 — 现实感官”的关系反转，
尝试以完全的“数字感官”感知真实存在的现实内容。
通过运用软硬件串联，AI机器学习分类决策等方法制作可实际运行的多模态感知设备。
In this media perception course, we reverse the relationship between "digital content-sense of reality" and try to perceive the reality of reality with a complete "digital sense". Through the use of software and hardware in series, AI machine learning classification and decision-making methods, a multi-modal sensing device that can actually operate is made.

《诺亚部落》将重新审视生物感知的产生，以及在后媒介的当代设计语境下，
对植根于未来特殊环境中具有独特媒介性的感知方式展开实验。
"Noah Tribe" will re-examine the generation of biological perception, and in the context of post-media contemporary design, it will conduct experiments on the unique media perception rooted in the special environment of the future.

三大增强感知系统剖析  

Analysis of three enhanced perception systems

01.

生物方位
触觉感知系统.

Biological orientation
tactile perception system.

我们通过基于OpenCV的实时摄像跟踪人脸，由AI监督机器学习标记训练数据并输出为不同的分类决策，最终实现对人位置灵敏的触觉感知跟踪。佩戴者仅通过穿戴设备上不同位置的低频振动马达即能感知到身边靠近的人的具体位置并与之主动对话

We track human faces through real-time cameras based on OpenCV, and AI-supervised machine learning to label training data and output them as different classification decisions, finally realizing tactile tracking that is sensitive to human positions. The wearer can perceive the specific location of the person close to him and actively dialogue with it only through low-frequency vibration motors in different positions on the wearable device

 

02.

方位立体声
感知系统.

Azimuth stereo
Perception system.

参与者一旦靠近装置,超声波传感器收到肢体动作的距离反馈后将自动调节鲸鱼音频的音效。所有距离数据均由数字编码实时处理。

Once the participant approaches the device, the ultrasonic sensor
will automatically adjust the sound effect of the whale audio after receiving the distance feedback of the body movement. All
distance data are processed in real time by digital coding.

03.

数据化
视觉系统.

Digital vision system


在这个装置里,我们通过超声波发射器运用时间差测距法将所监测到的距离信息传入Arduino,经过运算学习,分别传入对应左前、正前、右前方位的LED屏中.

In this device, we use the ultrasonic transmitter to use the time difference ranging method to transfer the monitored distance information to the Arduino, and after calculation and learning, it is passed into the LED screens corresponding to the front left, front,
and front right positions.
